Sablés Recipe

Description

Sablés (This is a french word for this type of cookies, "sable" means "sand" in english and refers to the texture inside the cookies, feels like broken into sand in the mouth), ia a delicious piece of pastry that you can take as a snack on the go, or include in your dessert or even in your breakfast meal. Reach in fats and fibers, the taste puts you in delight mode. The beautiful goldenish look gives it a prestigious look when dressed on the table.

Here are the ingredients needed and steps for making this recipe as a chef.

Ingredients

  • 300g of wheat flour
  • 150g of butter
  • 30Cl of milk
  • 100g of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Take the butter out of the fridge and wait for it to unfreeze
  2. Put floor a recipient
  3. Add Sugar, milk and egg and a pinch of salt
  4. Add butter
  5. Mix everything
  6. Put your mixture in the fridge for an hour
  7. Using a rolling pin to make your mixture flat
  8. Preheat the Oven at 180°C
  9. Use a cutter of your desired shape and size to cut into many pieces
  10. Put your cut pieces on a sheet pan and bake for 15 mins

    11. Enjoy!
