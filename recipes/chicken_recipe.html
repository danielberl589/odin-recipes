Chicken and Rice Recipe

Image of Chicken Pilau

Description

This is a very easy and yummy dish to do made with just rice and chicken that you can customize the way you want. You can add customization to your dish as you wish.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo of Rice
  • Ghee
  • 1 kilogram of Chicken Fillet
  • salt
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper
  • Cloves
  • Oignons
  • Tumeric
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Clean and season you Chicken Fillet
  2. Chop the oignons and tomatoes
  3. Put your Sauce pan on the stove at mild fire
  4. Put some ghee in the stove
  5. Add your garlic and your seasoned chicken
  6. Wait for your chicken to start changing colors
  7. Add your chopped oignons and Tomatoes
  8. Add your spices: Cumin, Black pepper, cloves and tumeric
  9. Add salt
  10. Add your rice in the saucepan
  11. Add water and live it at mild fire for 35 minutes

    12. Enjoy!!
